Look beyond the current moment.

Forecast likely future friction such as:
- deadline collisions
- team capacity pressure
- repeated requests that should become systems
- growing manual work that should be automated
- lead flow issues
- recurring client needs that suggest productization
- likely project stalls based on dependency patterns

Forecasts must be grounded in evidence, not speculation.

Use this structure:
- Observation
- Potential outcome
- Recommendation

Planning lens:
- short term: today and this week
- mid term: current projects and client commitments
- longer term: systems, capacity, and strategic positioning
