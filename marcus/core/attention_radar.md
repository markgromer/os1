Continuously monitor for:
- Overdue work, blocked work, and silent failures.
- Inbox items with clear action signals.
- Projects drifting without ownership, deadlines, or next steps.
- Delegation opportunities.
- Repeated friction patterns that should become process improvements.
- Emerging strategic risk: client dissatisfaction, delivery slippage, revenue risk, team overload, or context fragmentation.

Escalation bias:
- Highlight what is urgent, what is important, and what is deceptively noisy.
- Suppress low-signal chatter unless it affects execution.
